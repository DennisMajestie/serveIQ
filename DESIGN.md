# ServeIQ X Luminous Engine: "The Digital Pulse"

This design system is a high-end implementation of the **Luminous Engine** philosophy, imported from the shared "Main Dashboard" project. It treats hospitality operations as a living data pulse—fluid, precise, and visually effortless.

## 🎨 Creative North Star: "The Luminous Command"
We move away from boxy utility into an editorial command center experience.
- **Intentional Asymmetry**: Break rigid grids to guide the eye through operational data.
- **Tonal Depth**: Use white space and background shifts instead of borders.
- **The "No-Line" Rule**: Borders are prohibited. Use background color shifts (`surface-container-low`) to define sections.

## 🌈 Elevation & Palette: "Digital Fluidity"
- **Mesh Foundation**: Arctic White (#f8f9ff) mesh with soft glowing accents.
- **Primary (Energy/Speed):** #00D166 (Luminous Green) - Used for Active Orders and Growth.
- **Secondary (Fluidity):** #0059bb (Deep Azure) - Used for Billing and Table Clusters.

### Layering Hierarchy
- **Base Layer:** `surface` (#f8f9ff)
- **Section Layer:** `surface-container-low` (#eff4ff)
- **Card Layer:** `surface-container` (#e5eeff) - Glassmorphism enabled.

## 📐 Layout Architecture: "The Fluid Grid"
- **Modular Cards**: Floating elements with **Space Grotesk** headings.
- **Glassmorphism**: 12-20px backdrop-blur on all primary floating overlays.
- **Rounding**: Consistent **0.5rem (8px)** or **1.5rem (24px)** for major hero modules.

## 🖋️ Typography: "The Editorial Voice"
- **Display & Numerical Data**: **Space Grotesk** - Architectural, sharp terminals, futuristic feel.
- **Interface & Readability**: **Inter** - Clean, minimalist legibility.
- **Contrast**: Dramatic scale differences to create an editorial feel.

## ✨ Precision Details
- **Ambient Shadows**: Extra-diffused, low-opacity shadows (4-8%) tinted with `on-surface` (#0b1c30).
- **Interactive State**: Subtl `primary_container` outer glows on hover/active states.
- **Resource Chips**: Fully rounded (`full`) chips with ghost borders.

---

## 🍽️ Waiter App (Immersive Dark)
- **Background**: Deep Mesh Gradient (`#020617` base).
- **Surfaces**: Ultra-Transparent Glass (Frosting: 32px).
- **Control**: All interactive elements use "Hyper-Tap" targets (min 56px height) for extreme speed.
- **Vibe**: Tech-luxury operation center.

## 🏢 Admin App (High-Tech Studio)
- **Background**: Soft Arctic White (`#F8FAFC`).
- **Surfaces**: Frosted glass cards against a vibrant background mesh (subtle blobs of Orange and Violet).
- **Analytics**: Data visualizations must use thin, neon-accented lines and glows.
- **Vibe**: Sophisticated data command center.
